Ooty (Udhagamandalam)

  1. Known as the "Queen of Hill Stations," Ooty is nestled in the Nilgiri Hills.
  2. Features stunning landscapes, including tea plantations, botanical gardens, and the picturesque Ooty Lake.
  3. Home to the famous Nilgiri Mountain Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  4. Offers pleasant weather throughout the year, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.
  5. Provides opportunities for trekking, boating, wildlife spotting, and exploring the local tribal culture.

Kodaikanal

  1. Situated amidst the Palani Hills, Kodaikanal is known for its serene beauty and pleasant climate.
  2. Offers breathtaking viewpoints like Coaker's Walk, Dolphin's Nose, and Pillar Rocks.
  3. Home to the Kodaikanal Lake, where visitors can enjoy boating and serene walks along the promenade.
  4. Features the unique Shola forests, which are rich in biodiversity and offer fantastic trekking opportunities.
  5. Known for its homemade chocolates, aromatic spices, and eucalyptus oil, which make excellent souvenirs.

Yercaud

  1. Located in the Eastern Ghats, Yercaud is a charming hill station often referred to as the "Jewel of the South."
  2. Offers stunning viewpoints like Lady's Seat, Pagoda Point, and the Telescope Observatory for panoramic views of the valley.
  3. Known for its coffee plantations, orange groves, and spice gardens.
  4. Features the Emerald Lake, where visitors can enjoy boating and peaceful walks around its serene shores.
  5. Provides opportunities for trekking, camping, and exploring the Montfort School, known for its architectural beauty.
