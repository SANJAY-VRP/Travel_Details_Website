Sirumalai

  1. Situated in the Eastern Ghats, Sirumalai is a lesser-known hill station famous for its natural beauty and pleasant climate.
  2. Offers scenic viewpoints like Sirumalai View Tower and Perumal Peak, providing panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
  3. Known for its coffee plantations, organic farming, and honey production.
  4. Features numerous waterfalls, such as Suruli Falls and Kutladampatti Falls, offering refreshing escapades.
  5. Provides opportunities for trekking, birdwatching, and exploring the Sirumalai Reserved Forest.
